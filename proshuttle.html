The TICO PRO-SHUTTLE 45TM 70 Ton Terminal Trailer

TICO Terminal Trailer – value that counts to the bottom line in your Real World. Ease of maintenance and maximum up time are greatly increased with these TICO Pro-Shuttle Terminal Trailer benefits.

  • Uses bottom bolt-on king-pins to reduce wear and increase lifespan.
  • Utilizes the proprietary cornerless design pioneered by TICO and has shorter, more aggressively pitched guides. These guides allow for the container to be guided into the trailer without any hang-up and misalignments, further reducing damage to the lower rails of the container. The rear guide allows for excellent access to open the rear doors or visually inspect security seals from ground level. By allowing deflection of the guides by the full weight of a container, possible damage to structural floor members is eliminated.
  • Provides a landing leg system that is mounted directly to the bottom of the main beams. This transfers the weight of a loaded trailer dropped from the tractor directly on the main beams and not on the side of the main beams. Also, the landing leg that has an angled leading support allows for the leg to take a glancing blow and hop over any impact on the ground.
  • Ensures that safety is the benchmark of the Pro-Shuttle as the cut-out areas have Patent Pending Man-Savers™ angles to reduce injury if the operator accidentally moves.
  • Provides Patent Pending No-Harm™ Pneumatic Guides that are controlled from the left side of the trailer and allow independent deployment.
  • Uses smooth surface that allow the steel to steel contact to slide instead of catch, eliminating damage to the trailer and lift. The smooth side rail also aids in eliminating damage to the masts of top handlers and empty handlers when drivers pull away from the lift and the mast is against the side rail.
  • Utilizes real world tire design with aperture holes in the side wall that greatly reduce heat build-up while giving better deflection than standard “soft ride” solid tires.
